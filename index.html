CHILD MARRIAGES

A team of women from different cultures and backgrounds coming together to merge tech with health related issues to be able to solve the problem of our society inclusive and majorly on Early Childhood Marriage, on both young girls and boys.

This is a startup up company, by our very own AO who felt the need to try and find a solution to ensure both young girls and boys do not get married at tender ages.

We will serve the good of the society and want to ensure that kids get Education and do not become scarred for life.

We will try to offer the possible solution to this problem;

  • Communities to be conducting teaching groups where the kids are able to be taught on how to protect themselves in case such a situation happens, either in the family or community.
  • Be able to create play groups for the kids.
  • Have a helpline number to ensure the cases are reported before, during and even after they happen.

This is a starting company and we are hoping, to grow better and be better with time.
